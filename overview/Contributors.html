Urdacha Contributors

Urban Data Challenge - Ur-Da-Cha.

Or, as some would say, Your Russian County Home. Bringing together data wranglers, computer software experts, and urban planning enthousiasts together. Had you biked around London in the 70s, you might have run into Tim or Theo. If you bike around SF or Oakland these days, you might run into any of us!

Cynthia Armour: urban planning enthusiast, bicycle advocate, and general fan of well-planned cities and transportation networks. Cynthia's role in Urdacha is that of Project Manager - establishing the course, getting things done, and giving pep talks.

Tim Child: Tim is the CEO of Silicon Valley start-up that is focusing on using GPU accelerated databases for rich content management systems. He previously held Vice President of Engineering positions at Oracle Corporation, Informix Inc. and BEA Systems Inc.; where he led teams that developed advanced database, 3D graphics and web applications. This includes large database systems for the NASA EOSDIS project, Navteqs vehicle navigation systems and the Government of New Zealand LINZ (Land Information New Zealand) system. Tim is the data explorer and database expert for Urdacha.

Theo Armour: Theo has been actively involved with developing methods for displaying quantitative data using animation and 3D, in particular with using webgl and three.js. Theo is a data wrangler, data explorer, and data visualizer for the Urdacha team.
