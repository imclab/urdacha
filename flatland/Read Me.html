flatLand ~ Read Me

These 'maps' load all San Francisco stops and every current vehicle positions and movement directly off the Nextbus web site. For the moment the presentation is highly abstracted: There is a semi-transparent surface, with stops positioned under the surface and buses above.

Future edits that would improve the readability and use of the map:

  • Indicate bus directions
  • Built-in updating
  • Display stops and buses for any of the 20+ Nexbus agencies
  • Mouse over a bus or stop will display heads-up particulars
  • Replay the day's schedule - 24 hours in a minute or two
  • Shadows - to indicate current time of day
  • Performance improvements and bug fixes
